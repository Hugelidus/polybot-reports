⚠️ Methodological correction since v2

The bug: v2 stratified by volume_usd from markets.parquet, which is the FINAL total volume at market close. This is post-hoc info — at the moment of any given rise event, only ~10% of that final volume had been traded (median).

Why it leaked: Yes-farmers and arbitrageurs flood markets in the final hours/days before resolution, inflating volume on the WINNING side. Bigger final volume = correlated with dramatic outcomes = correlated with side_won. So the bucket "final volume >$10M" was contaminated with markets where the outcome was already known.

Magnitude of the leak: Median ratio (vol_at_event / vol_final) = 9.7%. Mean = 15.2%. That means ~85-90% of the final volume happened AFTER the rise we were analyzing.

Updated conclusions for production

What to actually deploy

  1. Trigger: rise from <low to >high on a side, low ≤ 0.30, high ≤ 0.40 (mid-band).
  2. Buy filter: EXP_HIGH+MID ≥ 50% of buy USD share AND n_wallets ≥ 4 (combined: +24.3% pooled ROI on >$1M vol_at; cleaner signal in $10K-100K).
  3. Skip filter (fade): if FRESH+NEW+SEMI ≥ 50% of buy USD → DO NOT TRADE (loses universally).
  4. Volume guard (at-time): require cum_vol_at_event_ts ≥ $10K AND ≤ $1M. Below: too thin / too noisy. Above: chaotic, less reliable.
  5. Hold strategy: buy at price = high, hold to resolution. (Future work: model take-profit / stop-loss.)

Realistic ROI expectation

  • Per trade: +5% to +15% net of slippage and fees (after the leakage correction).
  • Best subset (low entry, sweet vol): +20-30% per trade theoretical, +15-20% net.
  • Total available events/year: approximately 3,000-5,000 across all robust cells.

Caveats

  • Hold time: median 6-9 days. Capital lockup matters for sizing.
  • Liquidity at entry price = high needs verification per cell. Bigger markets: better.
  • Holdout = 2.5 months only. Multi-fold CV would harden the conclusions.
  • The wallet at-time classification (nm_at) requires real-time tracking infrastructure.
